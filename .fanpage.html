The Biography of a Queen

RuPaul Andre Charles was born on November 17, 1960 in San Diego, although at the age of 15 moved to Atlanta to study performing arts. Rupaul made his rise to fame in the Georgia night club scene where he started performing musically and in drag.
Then in 1993 RuPaul made his first hit!
Through rap label "Tommy Boy" RuPaul recorded his first album Supermodel of the World. The hit dance track "Supermodel (You Better work)" came off the album to a surprising success on MTV channels.
The song even hit number two on the Billboard Hot Dance Music/Club Play chart!

The Queen of the Dance Track

RuPaul has released twelve studio albums and fourty-five singles. To this day RuPaul continues to make music that are still being played in dance clubs around the globe.

The Queen Behind the Makeup

While RuPaul is, of course, most well known for his musical appearances in drag, he has actually done much more! From talk shows to reality T.V. shows RuPaul has done it all!
Even today his hit reality TV show RuPaul's Drag Race is already confirmed for an eleventh season with an All Stars spinoff going on five seasons
He also does a weekly podcast called What's The Tea! Whether he is recording his new talk show or his hit TV program, he is also constantly advocating for LGBTQ+ rights across the world!
